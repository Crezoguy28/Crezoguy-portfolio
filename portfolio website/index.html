Hello! I'm Abhijeet Kumawat

known by my creative identity Crezoguy — the visionary behind Crezography.  Crezography isn’t just a name; it’s a mindset. The word blends “Crezo” (inspired by “crazy” for bold, mind-blowing creativity) with “graphy” — representing everything I do: graphic design, photography, cinematography, and beyond.

As a passionate UI/UX designer, storyteller, and visual creator, I design experiences that are not only functional but emotionally engaging. From mobile apps and dashboards to cinematic visuals and brand identities, I approach every project with imagination, purpose, and precision.
